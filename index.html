    towers of hanoi

    rules

The Towers of Honoi is a mathemaetical game or puzzle consisting of three rods and a number of disks of various diameters.
Only one disk may be moved at a time.
Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stack and placing it on top of another stack or on empty rod.
No disk may be placed on the top of the disk that is small than other.
